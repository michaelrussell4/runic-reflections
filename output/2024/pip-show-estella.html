Pip Show Estella

For her saccharine beauty
His lugubrious lustful craving
Quixotic pursuits and hubris
An earnest for her taking

Sycophants make her entourage
They swarm around the queen
Social facade and barrage
Warm drones sustain her sheen

His sartorial tastes impress
His cool hides desparation
She flaunts her unctuousness
Her cost: his highest oblation

To gain her glance, an onerous task
Her heart, a futility
For her love's an illusion
While her status is sure
His bluff is the latter
While his former is pure

Commentary

A little poem entitled with reference to Pip from Great Expectations and playing with his name by referencing pip, a python programming tool for installing packages. Pip, from Dicken's Great Expectations, pursues the unattainable Estella, a girl reared to punish man by luring him in and offering only an empty heart. Pip tries and fails.
