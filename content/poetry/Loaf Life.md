Title: Loaf Life 🍞
Date: 2026/03/04 02:18PM
Category: Poetry
Tags: comical, educational

> Here's a cool rule you don't learn in school<br>
> Fine time for a treat you won't find it on the street<br>
> This beat I'm cookin' got everybody lookin'<br>
> Fermentation creation; a healthy taste sensation<br>
> I'm sour every hour but it's yummy in my tummy<br>
> You don't know what I said—I'll tell you it's bread<br>
> Life with no strife cuz I make it for my wife<br>
> Slap jelly make it sweet toast it up with some heat<br>
> Sing about this thing we go from summertime to spring<br>
> Pack it for snack, attack it, take a crack<br>
> Smack smack gobble that; and now we sing it back<br>
> Glycemic index low; sour bread you know<br>
> Good for the gut; tastes good, tell you what
