God Of Heaven

God of Heaven wilt Thou hear my prayer
  To Thee I kneel in supplication
    Let Thine eyes in mercy see
      My earnest pleas upward ascending
    Let Thy love consume my heart
  To Thee I bow in worship now
God of Heaven Thou are holy

God my Father forgive me thy child
  With open hands receive me
    For Christ has paid with precious blood
      His submissive condescension
    For Christ obeyed Thy command
  With wounded hands as His proof
God my Father tears for joy abound

God of Holiness reign eternal
  To Thy throne we send our praise
    For we were lost and scattered then
      Eternal hands encircled us
    For to gather and to raise
  To Thy throne of divinity
God of Holiness glory to Thee

Meaning & commentary

There are three verses and each is a chiasma following the form A-B-C-D-C-B-A where each letter corresponds to a repeated theme in the line.
