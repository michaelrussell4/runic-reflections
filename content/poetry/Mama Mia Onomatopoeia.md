Title: Mama Mia Onomatopoeia
Date: 2024/09/05 12:53PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: whimsical, childrens, logophile

> - The buzz of the bee
> - Who makes our honey
> - The sizzling sausage
> - Eaten by Mr. Gossage
> - The bang of a bomb
> - To disrupt the calm
> - The drip of a drop
> - Of rain from up top
> - The clang of a cowbell
> - Worn by old Marybell
> - The popping of popcorn
> - All yellow and warm
> - The crash of a car
> - It bent that bar
> - The splash of a seal
> - Letting out a squeal
> - The thud of a thing
> - That's heavy and green
> - The gurgling gulp
> - Of orange juice with pulp
> - The whirring wind
> - That makes me bend
> - The ding of a doorbell
> - It sounds very well
