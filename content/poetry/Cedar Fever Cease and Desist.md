Title: Cedar Fever Cease and Desist
Date: 2026/01/16 02:52PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: nature, satirical, seasonal, whimsical

> - Dear Mountain Cedar,
> - Ancient and strong you are it's true
> - In soil so dry it's amazing you grew
> - Your women are fair—blue berries in their hair
> - I wish this was all that I had to share
> - Forgive me now, but to be forthright
> - Your men have a habit which causes spite
> - On cold wintry days when the winds pick up
> - Their poisonous bombs begin to blow up
> - A yellow smoke of haze abounds
> - Then coughs and sneezes are heard all around
> - For this cause I bring to your attention
> - A complaint to resolve—intention for prevention
> - I beg you now—cease and desist
> - With best regards, I must insist
><br>
> - — the human folk
