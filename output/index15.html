  2. Seeing Through A Glass Darkly

    My reflection in darkness beckons my entrance
    With shimmering pieces of mystery there
    Far and unknown myself I am
    My relationships tie to veiled places
    Are they hidden or are they forgotten?
    Is knowledge lost or intentionally hid?
    Alas, all is vanity, vanity, vanity, I pity.

  3. Silent Dark Serenity

    Her lucent rays upon me lay
    Enshrouded by her silent sway
    A lustrous light on loch and glen
    Still the night and fears of men
    Lulling, calming, limpid strokes
    Smiling softly: a vagal coax
    Thank you mother, lunar light
    Silent spirit of the night
