Title: The Pedant's Last Plea
Date: 2026/05/07 15:19PM
Category: Poetry
Tags: comical, educational, logophile, satirical, whimsical

> Ye who cause such suffering and woe
> Wot ye not whereof your throw?
> Wherefore cast ye carelessly?
> I trow you speak heedlessly
> Watch, I pray, your wanton tongue
> Behold the damage you have done
> Ye haply shoot forth daggers red
> Would ye fain send them to bed?
> For what, ye wonder, durst I accuse?
> I tell you now lest anon ye bruise
> Catachresis, linguistic barbarism,
> Acyrology, and solecism
> At this the crowd was piqued
> "Sorcery, dark arts" they shrieked
> At the stake he burned alone
> This then was written on his stone:
> "Here lieth linguistic conservatism"
> "Who taketh with him his neologism"
> "His anger roused to break a trend"
> "Which rule he broke, which was his end"
