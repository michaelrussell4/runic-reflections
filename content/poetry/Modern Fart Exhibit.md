Title: Modern Fart Exhibit
Date: 2026/06/12 01:29PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: philosophical, nostalgic, melancholic, reflective

> - I stepped into the gallery
> - To see fine art from history
> - But to my shock I could not find
> - Any pieces of such kind
> - I saw some spills and noise confused
> - Some canvas much abused
> - But nothing worthy of the eye
> - Only chaos to defy
> - "Are there some who value this?"
> - "Or is it I who am amiss?"
> - I wondered with scrunched brow
> - Why these things were here and how
> - Why would someone value these
> - What look like remnants of a sneeze
> - What happened to the skillful hands
> - Which painted smooth skies over luscious lands
> - Where are the works of expert mind
> - Intricate detail, well defined
> - Why can't I find the master's creations
> - Which well relate the height of nations
> - Alas, modernity, I'm afraid
> - Has diverged and from beauty strayed
