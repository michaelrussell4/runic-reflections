Title: Mister E Johnwithanee
Date: 2026/06/01 11:25AM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: melancholic, reflective, existential, comical

> - My name is Mister E Johnwithanee.
> - My life includes great difficulty.
> - I live at the cross of Road st and Bomb.
> - Email me at dot.TheWordAT@email.com
> - I was born February 29th, two-thousand and zero.
> - The phone number for Hungry Caballero
> - Happens to be my social security number.
> - My sleep talk's offensive—and I often slumber.
> - I'm narcoleptic and have halitosis.
> - My identity is itself a prognosis.
> - So, dear reader, count yourself lucky
> - You aren't this poor man from Kentucky.
