Title: Red Faced Tom
Date: 2025/01/14 12:19PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: narrative, logophile, comical

> - Feckless Tom
> - Had a fecund wife
> - Having two large drupes
> - And a kitchen knife
> - 
> - Tom could hardly wait
> - For his meal at hand
> - For to masticate
> - He did then demand
> - 
> - But his niggardly ways
> - Left his wife quite cross
> - Gave a niggling gaze
> - His face a slap across

## Notes

Contrary to the thoughts of some, this poem is about a man who looks forward to eating a variety of [drupes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drupe), but who faces a stern rebuke of his wife after annoying her... 😅
