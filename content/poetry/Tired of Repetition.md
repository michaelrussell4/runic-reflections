Title: Tired Of Repetition
Date: 2024/07/12 02:33PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: melancholic, reflective, serious, existential

> - Maybe I need to sweep
> - Baby cries feed to sleep
> - Achy thighs lead to weep
> - Steady eyes plead to keep
> - Fading mind

## Meaning & Commentary

This exercise rhymes, or near-rhymes, every respective word of each line; e.g., maybe, baby, achy, steady; I, cries, thighs, eyes; need, feed, lead, plead; etc.; except the last line.
