Title: The Pedant's Last Plea
Date: 2026/05/07 15:19PM
Category: Poetry
Tags: comical, educational, logophile, satirical, whimsical

> Ye who cause such suffering and woe<br>
> Wot ye not whereof your throw?<br>
> Wherefore cast ye carelessly?<br>
> I trow you speak heedlessly<br>
> Watch, I pray, your wanton tongue<br>
> Behold the damage you have done<br>
> Ye haply shoot forth daggers red<br>
> Would ye fain send them to bed?<br>
> For what, ye wonder, durst I accuse?<br>
> I tell you now lest anon ye bruise<br>
> Catachresis, linguistic barbarism,<br>
> Acyrology, and solecism<br>
> At this the crowd was piqued<br>
> "Sorcery, dark arts" they shrieked<br>
> At the stake he burned alone<br>
> This then was written on his stone:<br>
> "Here lieth linguistic conservatism"<br>
> "Who taketh with him his neologism"<br>
> "His anger roused to break a trend"<br>
> "Which rule he broke, which was his end"
