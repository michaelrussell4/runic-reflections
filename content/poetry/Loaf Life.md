Title: Loaf Life 🍞
Date: 2026/03/04 02:18PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: whimsical, educational, childrens

> - Here's a cool rule you don't learn in school
> - Fine time for a treat you won't find it on the street
> - This beat I'm cookin' got everybody lookin'
> - Fermentation creation; a healthy taste sensation
> - I'm sour every hour but it's yummy in my tummy
> - You don't know what I said—I'll tell you it's bread
> - Life with no strife cuz I make it for my wife
> - Slap jelly make it sweet toast it up with some heat
> - Sing about this thing we go from summertime to spring
> - Pack it for snack, attack it, take a crack
> - Smack smack gobble that; and now we sing it back
> - Glycemic index low; sour bread you know
> - Good for the gut; tastes good, tell you what
