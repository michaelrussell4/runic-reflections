> - For her [saccharine](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/saccharine) beauty
> - His [lugubrious](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/lugubrious) lustful craving
> - [Quixotic](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Quixotic) pursuits and [hubris](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/hubris)
> - An [earnest](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/earnest) for her taking<br>
> - [Sycophants](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Sycophants) make her [entourage](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/entourage)

> - Warm [drones](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/drones) sustain her sheen<br>
> - His [sartorial](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sartorial) tastes impress
> - His cool hides desperation
> - She [flaunts](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/flaunts) her [unctuousness](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/unctuousness)
> - Her cost: his highest [oblation](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/oblation)<br>
> - To gain her glance, an [onerous](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/onerous) task
> - Her heart, a [futility](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/futility)
> - For her love's an [illusion](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/illusion)
> - While her status is sure
> - His [bluff](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/bluff) is the latter
> - While his former is pure

## Commentary

A little poem entitled with reference to Pip from Great Expectations and playing with his name by referencing `pip`, a python programming tool for installing packages. Pip, from Dicken's Great Expectations, pursues the unattainable Estella, a girl reared to punish man by luring him in and offering only an empty heart. Pip tries and fails.
