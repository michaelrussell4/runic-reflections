Title: Bridled Beast
Date: 2026/06/17 09:28PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: existential, philosophical, religious, abstract

> - She dislikes the crunching bones
> - Wincing in these pressing stones
> - She in scorching, fiery rain
> - Swelters in this searing bane
> - She detests the rancid air
> - Fainting in this devil's snare
> - He grinds his teeth with grit
> - Reigning with power and wit
> - He spews out flagrant drool
> - Exhaust from burning fuel
> - He breathes in battle flow
> - Flaring nostrils, strong winds blow
> - She is master of the beast
> - He must yield to her, the least
> - But both are used by man above
> - Will he drive with greed or love?

## Notes

The bit is oppressed by the horse yet controls him. The rider prevails over both. A metaphor is offered for bit, horse, rider: woman, man, and God.
