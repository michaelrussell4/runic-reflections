  5. Man Child

    Blithely impetuous
    And lacking temperance
    A cheeky demeanor
    Conceit, misdemeanor
    Erstwhile needy
    Now thankless, greedy
    A plague of arrogance
    Squandering inheritance
    Incorrigible enmity
    Tumultuous as sea
