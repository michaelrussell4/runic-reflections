Title: Dreamy Exercise
Date: 2026/02/24 04:45PM
Category: Poetry
Tags: reflective, surreal, whimsical

> A spark in the dark makes a fire warm<br>
> The moon makes a glow after a snowstorm<br>
> I dream I'm walking on a balance beam<br>
> While a tune is sung by an ugly goon<br>
> I fall through stars away to somewhere far<br>
> I fall deep as I continue to sleep<br>
> Dark as night but then a great light<br>
> I look up high and I see the sky
