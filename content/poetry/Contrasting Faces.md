Title: Contrasting Faces
Date: 2026/06/29 06:18PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: melancholic, existential, philosophical, reflective

> - The bird and the fox at play today
> - Pitting the mind at the heart
> - Sitting in misery withering soul
> - Will there be beauty or gray?
> - <br>
> - The fox, in the latter is grounded
> - The bird, a vibrant flight
> - But both cannot be—two disagree
> - Torn by this terrible plight
> - <br>
> - Look in father's mirror, Orual
> - See your reflected soul
> - She pines for your love, she pines
> - Quench with the water of her bowl
> - <br>
> - The fowl bird dazzled with beauty
> - On Istra he painted by hand
> - The fine fox crafted with words
> - Leaving seer and seen both ugly
