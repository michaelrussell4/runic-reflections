Title: The Pedant's Last Plea
Date: 2026/05/07 03:19PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: logophile, narrative, satirical, serious

> - Ye who cause such suffering and [woe](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/woe)
> - [Wot](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Wot) ye not whereof your throw?
> - Wherefore cast ye carelessly?
> - I [trow](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/trow) you speak [heedlessly](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/heedlessly)
> - Watch, I [pray](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pray), your [wanton](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/wanton) tongue
> - Behold the damage you have done
> - Ye [haply](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/haply) shoot forth daggers red
> - Would ye [fain](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/fain) send them to bed?
> - For what, ye wonder, [durst](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/durst) I accuse?
> - I tell you now [lest](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/lest) [anon](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/anon) ye bruise
> - [Catachresis](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Catachresis), [barbarism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbarism_(linguistics)),
> - [Acyrology](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/acyrology), and [solecism](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/solecism)
> - At this the crowd was [piqued](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/piqued)
> - "Sorcery, dark arts" they shrieked
> - At the stake he burned alone
> - This then was written on his stone:
> - "Here lieth linguistic conservatism"
> - "Who taketh with him his [neologism](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/neologism)"
> - "His anger [roused](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/roused) to break a trend"
> - "Which rule he broke, which was his end"
