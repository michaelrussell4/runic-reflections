He Used Them All

A phantasm containing the paladin of hope
Awakening hope from the bogarting trope
Whose raucous tyranny extirpates life
His capricious lust we met with strife
Tawny remains, vapid and dead were produced
After putrid perfumes were by him induced
Such punctilious precision at work
Devastating agrarian fortune with a smirk
Voracious his shtick and meager our aegis
A dilapidated land and our dolorous wages
Now at the crepuscular, lenitive view
My weary soul inveigled by its pulchritude

A imaginery figure containing the leader of hope

Whose rude and violent dominance destroys life
His whimsical lust we met with strife

After rotten perfumes were by him induced
Such meticulous precision at work
Devastating farm fortune with a smirk
Deadly his traits and humble our protection
A destroyed and deteriorated land and our sorrwful wages
