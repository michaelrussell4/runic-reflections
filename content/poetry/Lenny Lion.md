Title: Lenny Lion
Date: 2026/02/24 04:47PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: childrens, comical, whimsical

> - Lenny Lion's lollypop
> - Glistened like a lemondrop
> - He licked the top nonstop
> - While walking to the bookshop
> - But someone shouted "Lion stop"
> - "No candy in the bookshop"
> - But Lenny Lion listened not
> - "I'll lick my little lollypop"
> - This lousy lazy lion laughed a lot
> - But Librarian llama Lilly
> - Didn't think it very silly
> - With a snort in a bout
> - She sent him flying out
