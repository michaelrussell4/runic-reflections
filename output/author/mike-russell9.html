  1. He Used Them All

    A phantasm containing the paladin of hope
    Awakening hope from the bogarting trope
    Whose raucous tyranny extirpates life
    His capricious lust we met with strife
    Tawny remains, vapid and dead were produced
    After putrid perfumes were by him induced
    Such punctilious precision at work
    Devastating agrarian fortune with a smirk …

  3. Humor And Pain

    When what one sought
    Has come to naught
    Or life is fraught
    With what ought not

    One cries their eyes
    Or laughs with sighs
    Why on this wise
    Do such arise?

    Ironic fate
    Brings joy or hate
    As curses great
    Or jokes relate

    Perhaps a tear
    From pain or cheer …

  5. Internal Torment

    Can vinegar bitter bite my tongue
    Purge my heart from dirt and dung
    Prefer its taste to salt or sweet
    Mind sits still ill desire delete

    Surge of fear that I might falter
    Give me a coal from off the altar
    I do as I want which thing I hate …
