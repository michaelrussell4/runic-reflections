Title: Trifling Quale
Date: 2025/02/19 08:09PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell

> The human being: wretched man<br>
> Not for well being: his program<br>
> Survive and breed: his duty bound<br>
> His pleading voice: a minor sound
