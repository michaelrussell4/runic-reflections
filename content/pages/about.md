## How the Site Works

I built **Runic Relics** using [Pelican](https://getpelican.com/), a static site generator that converts simple Markdown files into a polished website. Content I write begins as a Markdown file, allowing me to focus on the words themselves. A simple 
<span class="inline-block bg-gray-200 text-gray-700 font-mono text-sm px-2 rounded-md">
