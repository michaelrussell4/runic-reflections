Title: Hard To Cook Fish
Date: 2024/07/12 02:33PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: narrative, comical, childrens, whimsical

> - A pair of fish, Jim caught him two
> - "Well with these fish know what I'll do?"
> - "Stick 'em in a pot and make a stew"
> - Not fish but stones—old Jim mistook them
> - Took them home and then he cooked them
> - Biting down his teeth fell out
> - That’s when he discovered rocks ain't trout
