Title: A Maternal Touch
Date: 2024/07/12 02:33PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: reflective, serious, philosophical, existential

<img src="{static}/images/A Maternal Touch.png" class="poem-img">

> - Solace and beauty wouldn't be had
> - Nor the safety and calm of heart
> - If it weren't for maternity's presence
> - Were her gentle soft touch to depart
> - She's a refuge from sorrow
> - A balm for our pain
> - She's a flower in a field
> - A trust, a love no one could feign
