A Big and Beautiful Bear

Poor Callisto, you're stuck in a rut
Stewn in the sky with a cup for a butt
Once beheld as a beautiful sight
Now you're an ursine beast of the night
Lustful eyes cloaked with disguise
Jupiter's deeds were your demise
Once your fairness love brought forth
Now wayfarers your rump guides north
Whether by gods or man or cow
You were looked on then and still are now
