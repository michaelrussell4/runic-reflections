Title: Pushing Boundaries
Date: 2026/06/29 09:00PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: narrative, educational, serious, reflective

> - There was a fence put up
> - On one side stood a cliff
> - The kids played on the other
> - It stayed quite firm and stiff
> - <br>
> - But some were not content
> - The fence was in too much
> - The pushed it 'til it bent
> - It needed a fixing touch
> - <br>
> - The man came and repaired it
> - But now an inch on over
> - Kids in foolish folly
> - Shoved it and they shoulder'd
> - <br>
> - They toppled over pell-mell
> - They tumbled and they fell
