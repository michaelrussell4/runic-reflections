## Notes

In this piece I explore the hermeneutic tension implicit in modern systemic epistemology and suggest what conclusions can be dubiously asserted by the denial of existential supression. It's an apparent expression of the dysfunction riddled by conformant subservience with which we're all too well acustomed.
