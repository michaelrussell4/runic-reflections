Title: A Curious Taste
Date: 2026/06/29 08:39PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: reflective, philosophical, serious

> - A child prefers the simple or sweet
> - A nuget of chicken
> - Some mac'n'cheese
> - An ice-cream and chocolate treat
> - <br>
> - But how we age and grow, you know
> - Tastes will alter
> - For some things strange
> - The winds of change sure blow
> - <br>
> - Vinegar dressings, fermentation
> - Pickles, sprouts
> - Oysters, and fish
> - Even the ugly creeping crustacean
> - <br>
> - This is not true merely for food
> - So, too, of art
> - Story, and ways
> - Peculiarities won't us elude
