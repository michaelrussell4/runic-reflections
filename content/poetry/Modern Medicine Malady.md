Title: Modern Medicine Malady
Date: 2026/06/24 01:16PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: religious, existential, philosophical, reflective

> - I'm tired I'm sore
> - They require more
> - I want some rest
> - With stress I'm pressed
> - The world will declare
> - I need self care
> - I need some time
> - I, me, mine
> - But He says rather than feed
> - To sacrifice ego, let go and cede
> - Save myself: imploding doom
> - Lose in Him: soul will bloom
