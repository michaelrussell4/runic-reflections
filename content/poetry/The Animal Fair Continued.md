Title: The Animal Fair Continued
Date: 2026/06/16 04:33PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: childrens, comical, whimsical

> - I went to the animal fair
> - The birds and the beasts were there
> - A fat racoon dug a hole with a spoon
> - For a tunnel to go to the hare
> - 
> - He forgot to turn at the trunk
> - Navigating instead to the skunk
> - Whose worry and fear could be smelt in the air
> - And the racoon went home cuz he stunk

## Notes

A continuation of the song, [Animal Fair](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Animal_Fair_(song)).
