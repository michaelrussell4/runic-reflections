Title: Six And Seven
Date: 2025/01/27 12:57PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: religious, narrative, serious, philosophical

> - In the beginning 'twas empty and dark
> - The Lord came forth to creation embark
> - Six full days of work and test
> - A seventh then to cease and rest
> - Man God raised in glorious light
> - Who by a tree fell into the dark night
> - Then God descended to the fallen race
> - Who by a tree raised and suffered disgrace
> - Through six wounds pinned fast and sure
> - A seventh then out from water did pour
> - The Lord lay in tomb empty and dark
> - 'till he rose again to new life embark
