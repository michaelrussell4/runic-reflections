Title: Dreamy Exercise
Date: 2026/02/24 04:45PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: surreal, abstract, narrative, fantasy

> - A spark in the dark makes a fire warm
> - The moon makes a glow after a snowstorm
> - I dream I'm walking on a balance beam
> - While a tune is sung by an ugly goon
> - I fall through stars away to somewhere far
> - I fall deep as I continue to sleep
> - Dark as night but then a great light
> - I look up high and I see the sky
