Title: Man The Dream
Date: 2024/07/12 02:33PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: existential, philosophical, reflective, abstract

> - Man is just as a dream
> - His beginning unknown
> - In confusion he roams
> - Reliably he will be
> - Wreaking hellish abomination
> - Or benevolent divinity
> - Bringing beauty, love, joy, and peace
> - Or fearful tremor abounding
> - His being will continue
> - On living he must go
> - His end may be sudden
> - As a recollection

## Meaning & Commentary

Man's experience is like a dream. He can bring about violence and evil or beauty and peace. But his species goes on living. Perhaps his end will be as awakening from a dream—sudden and surprising.
