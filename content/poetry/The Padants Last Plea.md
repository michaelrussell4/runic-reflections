Title: The Pedant's Last Plea
Date: 2026/05/07 03:19PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: comical, educational, logophile, satirical, whimsical

> Ye who cause such suffering and [woe](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/woe)<br>
> [Wot](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Wot) ye not whereof your throw?<br>
> Wherefore cast ye carelessly?<br>
> I [trow](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/trow) you speak [heedlessly](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/heedlessly)<br>
> Watch, I [pray](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pray), your [wanton](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/wanton) tongue<br>
> Behold the damage you have done<br>
> Ye [haply](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/haply) shoot forth daggers red<br>
> Would ye [fain](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/fain) send them to bed?<br>
> For what, ye wonder, [durst](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/durst) I accuse?<br>
> I tell you now [lest](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/lest) [anon](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/anon) ye bruise<br>
> [Catachresis](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Catachresis), [barbarism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbarism_(linguistics)),<br>
> [Acyrology](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/acyrology), and [solecism](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/solecism)<br>
> At this the crowd was [piqued](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/piqued)<br>
> "Sorcery, dark arts" they shrieked<br>
> At the stake he burned alone<br>
> This then was written on his stone:<br>
> "Here lieth linguistic conservatism"<br>
> "Who taketh with him his [neologism](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/neologism)"<br>
> "His anger [roused](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/roused) to break a trend"<br>
> "Which rule he broke, which was his end"
