Title: Ο θρύλος των φτερών
Date: 2020/03/16 01:52PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: fantasy, logophile, narrative, adventurous

> - Call to mind King Minnow the cruel
> - Who trapped a beast with a winding tool
> - A cage was made by a clever friend
> - Whose death was wanted in the end
> - <br>
> - Know this now I do relate
> - A fish I named to obfuscate
> - But look within the story told
> - And find the meaning for to hold
> - <br>
> - The genius of the cage above
> - Did lose a son whom he did love
> - The son to sun began ascending
> - Glory great to fate impending
> - <br>
> - Failing, falling, down below
> - Submerged beneath from head to toe
> - Dripping sea and father's tears
> - Carried home with such short years
> - <br>
> - This fallen angel in a hellish night
> - Awaked by the goddess of light
> - Save us from this oh great Pit
> - Escape this place and do not quit
> - <br>
> - From this temple of subjugation
> - Value my last propagation
> - I tell the way to this place on high
> - Outline the path on which you fly
> - <br>
> - At the first location you will find
> - Pyramids of several kind
> - Next is made of syllables three
> - The starting letter is how you see
> - <br>
> - Then a country with a fowl name
> - Thanksgiving time brings it fame
> - Flying on lies number four
> - LIBerate Yourself from wAr
> - <br>
> - Northmost country you’ll go to
> - Has a flag red yellow and blue
> - Then fly back to where you started
> - Trace your path and see what’s charted
> - <br>
> - Two things you’ll need before you leave
> - The symbol traced is one to cleave
> - The other you will find within
> - The country of our home and kin
> - <br>
> - Symbol and country make a pair
> - Use them to enter a hidden lair
> - Three relics found along your path
> - Prepare you for her evil wrath
