A Home

A
house
Is not a home
A place merely to roam
A home is more pleasant and good
The difference between them lies deeper than wood
A house is a shelter a covering overhead
A house is a place having pillow and bed
A home is a fortress from what is profane
A home it has warmth and love to sustain
A house will protect you enough to get by
A home is a foundation to flourish and fly
