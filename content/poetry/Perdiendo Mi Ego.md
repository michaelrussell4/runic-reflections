Title: Perdiendo Mi Ego
Date: 2024/09/04 04:16PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: religious, existential, reflective

> - With mi amigo Ego I tango
> - A clingy thing he won't let go
> - His weight I carry with strain
> - His sustenance is my pain
> - I used to think us great friends
> - But knew not to what awful ends
> - Our companionship costs me loads
> - My slow death his hunger forbodes
> - So I'm seeking to lose my Ego
> - To find a new Friend. Here we go

## Notes

### Matthew 10:39

> - He that findeth his life shall lose it: and he that loseth his life for my sake shall find it.
