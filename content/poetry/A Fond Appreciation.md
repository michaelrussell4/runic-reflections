Title: A Fond Appreciation
Date: 2026/01/05 09:48PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: romantic, reflective, religious, serious

> - I remarked to my wife, my dear
> - As for celebration and cheer
> - My precious, my friend, my love
> - How I've cherished this time, my dove
> - You've been at my side
> - We've laughed, we've cried
> - We've seen quite a lot
> - Much joy you have brought
> - How wonderful life is with you
> - Whether good or gloomy and blue
> - I prefer to spend it together
> - Let God us eternally tether
> - My angel and beloved wife
> - My joy, my heart, my life
> - Her response as she looked in my eyes
> - You know what she said? "Likewise"
