Title: Helpfully Unhelpful
Date: 2025/02/28 04:05PM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: comical, whimsical, narrative, satirical

<blockquote>
<ul>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Sir, can you not help me?</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">I'm not sure what you mean</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Of course, you do not have to</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">Your intentions are not keen</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">I won't say I don't want your help</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">Just tell me what's your plea!</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Now I see that I'm a bother</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">With this I must agree</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Well perhaps I might just ask</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">Oh do it if you must</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Will you, won't you, can't you, sir</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">You're earning my disgust</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Oh nevermind, the time has past</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">At this I'm quite relieved</span></li>
<li><span class="leading-none block text-left">Oh good, I'm glad; you are not mad</span></li>
<li><span class="pl-16 leading-none block text-right">I'm afraid you've been deceived</span></li>
</ul>
</blockquote>
