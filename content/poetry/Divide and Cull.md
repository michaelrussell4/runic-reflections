Title: Divide And Cull
Date: 2024/10/15 11:27AM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: philosophical, existential, abstract, reflective

> - A point, a circle, a sphere, a self
> - A start, a skin, an image within
> - A smart division creation therein
> - A worthy abode, a tabernacle
> - A worshipping mode, sphinx and jackal
> - A process repeated, a ritual
> - A sacrifice needed, habitual
> - A factory producing, lust inducing
> - A toxin of ego, self-seducing
> - A love, a hate, embrace and tear
> - A joy, a pain, I'm everywhere
> - A self-referential image I am
> - A fractal, a snake, a wolf and a lamb
> - A kiss or bite this tail before us
> - A meal implores us, ouroboros
> - A self, a sphere, a circle, a point
