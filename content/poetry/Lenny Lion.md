Title: Lenny Lion
Date: 2026/02/24 04:47PM
Category: Poetry
Tags: adventurous, comical, whimsical

> Lenny Lion's lollypop<br>
> Glistened like a lemondrop<br>
> He licked the top nonstop<br>
> While walking to the bookshop<br>
> But someone shouted "Lion stop"<br>
> "No candy in the bookshop"<br>
> But Lenny Lion listened not<br>
> "I'll lick my little lollypop"<br>
> This lousy lazy lion laughed a lot<br>
> But Librarian llama Lilly<br>
> Didn't think it very silly<br>
> With a snort in a bout<br>
> She sent him flying out
