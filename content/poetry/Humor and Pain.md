Title: Humor And Pain
Date: 2024/08/20 11:34AM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: reflective, philosophical, melancholic, existential

> - When what one sought
> - Has come to naught
> - Or life is fraught
> - With what ought not
> - <br>
> - One cries their eyes
> - Or laughs with sighs
> - Why on this wise
> - Do such arise?
> - <br>
> - Ironic fate
> - Brings joy or hate
> - As curses great
> - Or jokes relate
> - <br>
> - Perhaps a tear
> - From pain or cheer
> - The same compares
> - Throughout the years
> - <br>
> - A wretched laugh
> - A humorous cry
