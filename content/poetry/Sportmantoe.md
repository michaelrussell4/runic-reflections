Title: Sportmantoe
Date: 2026/05/26 11:57AM
Category: Poetry
Author: Mike Russell
Tags: comical, logophile, whimsical

> - It's likely not handy
> - Not used by any dandy
> - This word about feet
> - Those of the athlete
> - You see, it's a word
> - You likely haven't heard
> - I made it up just now
> - Please don't ask me how
> - A toe that's well sized
> - From frequent exercise
> - Such as the hammer throw
> - The word is sportmantoe

## Notes

Silly poem about a [portmanteau](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/portmanteau) [neologism](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/neologism).
